Detailed Description

Backward Differential Formulas.

\[ x^{t+1} = \left(\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \alpha_{i} x^{t-i}\right)+ \Delta t \beta v^{t+1}\\ v^{t+1} = \left(\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \alpha_{i} v^{t-i}\right)+ \Delta t \beta a^{t+1} \]

See also
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backward_differentiation_formula
