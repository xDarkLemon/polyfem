Detailed Description

Implicit Newmark-beta method.

\[ x^{t+1} = x^t + \Delta t v^t + \frac{\Delta t^2}{2}((1-2\beta)a^t + 2 \beta a^{t+1})\\ v^{t+1} = v^t + (1-\gamma)\Delta ta^t + \gamma \Delta ta^{t+1} \]

See also
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newmark-beta_method
